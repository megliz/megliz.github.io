Our Store

Local, unique treasure in the heart of Downtown Statesville.

GG's is located in the heart of Historic Downtown Statesville in a quaint century-old building owned and operated by Gloria Hager, crowned the "Queen of Statesville" by Mayor Costi Kutteh. GG's houses one of the most unique collections of art, frames and gifts in North Carolina.
